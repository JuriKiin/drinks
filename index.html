Alcoholic drinks.

Whiskey Sour

Ingredients

  • 1.5 oz bourbon
  • 1 oz lemon juice
  • 0.5 oz simple syrup

Instructions

  • Fill shaker with ice
  • Pour lemon juice, syrup, and bourbon into shaker
  • Shake well
  • Strain into glass
  • Peel a lemon rind and rub around the rim of the glass
  • Serve!

Ai-La Palu

Ingredients

  • 2 oz vodka
  • 1.5 oz cranberry juice
  • 0.5 oz simple syrup
  • 3 dashes hot sauce

Instructions

  • Add ice to mixing glass
  • Pour all ingredients into your mixing glass
  • Stir together
  • Strain into cocktail glass
  • Serve!

Moscow Mule

Ingredients

  • 1.5 oz vodka
  • 2 slice of lime
  • 4 oz ginger beer

Instructions

  • Add ice to your glass
  • Pour vodka and giner beer into your glass
  • Squeeze in lime juice from 1 slice
  • Garnish with lime slice
  • Serve!

B52 (Shot)

Ingredients

  • 0.25 oz Grand Marnier
  • 0.25 oz Coffee Liqueur
  • 0.25 oz Baileys Irish Cream

Instructions

  • Pour coffee liqueur into shot glass
  • Gently layer Baileys Irish Cream
  • Gently layer the Grand Marnier
  • Serve!

Non-alcoholic

Blueberry Mojito

Ingredients

  • 6-8 Mint Leaves
  • Handful of fresh blueberries
  • 1.75 oz of lime juice
  • 2 oz of simple syrup
  • Splash of club soda

Instructions

  • Fill glass with ice
  • Lightly Muddle blueberries
  • Add all ingredients and ice in a shaker.
  • Shake 15 seconds
  • Strain into glass
  • Serve!

Rosemary Blueberry Smash

Ingredients

  • 7-8 Blueberries
  • 1 Rosemary Sprig, stripped
  • 1 oz honey syrup
  • 1 oz fresh lemon juice
  • 4 oz Club Soda

Instructions

  • Gently muddle blueberries, rosemary, and honey in your shaker
  • Add lemon juice and ice, and shake for 10 seconds
  • Strain into glass with fresh ice
  • Top with club soda
  • Stir to incorporate
  • Serve!

Cucumber Giblet

Ingredients

  • 3 oz club soda
  • 4-5 slices of muddled cucumber
  • 1 oz fresh lime juice
  • 2 oz simple syrup

Instructions

  • Fill glass with ice
  • In your shaker, muddle cucumber slices
  • Add lime juice, simple syrup, and club soda to shaker
  • Add ice and shake for 15 seconds
  • Strain into glass
  • Serve!
